# Brand & Vision

## Motto / Vision (locked 2026-07-05)
> **Invisible payments — for you, and for your AI.**

This is the one line. It's the vision and the tagline. Use it everywhere (submission, banner, pitch).

## The vision, in full
Paying online is still clunky — and now it's not just *us* paying, it's the AI agents working for us too. People fight with wallets, cards, and logins. Agents hit a paywall and stop cold. Paying should disappear: you pay for anything with nothing but an email, and your AI pays its own way so it never gets stuck. Money should move as freely as everything else you do online.

## Product name — LOCKED: **Tab** (2026-07-05)
- The product/brand is **Tab**. "Leash" stays as the **agent-spending mode within Tab** ("your agent runs a Tab, on a Leash") — not a separate brand, never mashed into "Tab-Leash".
- Two ways to spend from one Tab: **you** check out with just an email; **your AI agents** pay their own way (Leash mode) on a budget you set and can pull anytime. One balance, anyone (or anything) can pay from it.
- Banner wordmark + submission title = **Tab**.
