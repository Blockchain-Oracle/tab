# Rules & Deliverables

> Source: `instuctions.md`.

## Mandatory deliverables (eliminated if missing)
- **Universal Accounts Track:** UA SDK in **EIP-7702 mode** + **≥1 cross-chain value-moving operation via UA** + a **functional demo** (deployed or runnable locally).
- **General Track:** a Web3 app with exceptional UX + a chosen **subtrack** (Openfort or ZeroDev).
- **Arbitrum bonus:** app + components **deployed on Arbitrum**, running **primarily on Arbitrum**.
- **Magic bonus:** **Magic embedded wallet** integrated.

## Rules
- **All work original and created during the hackathon. Plagiarism = disqualification.**
- Judges' decisions are final.
- **You keep full IP rights.**

## Incubation upside
Winning projects in the Particle tracks may, at Particle's discretion, be considered
for **incubation**. (Not just prize money — a possible accelerator path.)
