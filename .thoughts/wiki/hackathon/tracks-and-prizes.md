# Tracks & Prizes

> Source: `instuctions.md`. You submit to **one** main track; bonuses stack on top.

## Main tracks (pick ONE to submit)

### 🏆 Universal Accounts Track — flagship (Particle Network)
- **1st $2,500 · 2nd $2,000 · 3rd $1,500**
- Build a dApp that *prominently* uses Particle's **Universal Accounts SDK in EIP-7702 mode**, with a supported wallet provider, for chain-agnostic UX.
- Hard requirements:
  1. Must use Universal Accounts SDK **in EIP-7702 mode**.
  2. At least **one cross-chain operation moving value via UA**.
  3. Functional demo (deployed or runnable locally).
- Winners may be considered for **incubation by Particle Network**.

### 🏆 General Track (Particle Network)
- **1st $2,000 · 2nd $1,200 · 3rd $800**
- Any Web3 app with exceptional UX in any domain (payments, DeFi, AI, gaming, social).
- Bar: "seamless, intuitive, genuinely improved by the infra used."
- **Must also choose one subtrack:**
  - **Subtrack 1 — Openfort ($100)**
  - **Subtrack 2 — ZeroDev ($500)**
- Winners may also be considered for incubation.

## Bonus bounties (open to EITHER main track, judged independently)

### Arbitrum "Road to Open House London" — $2,000 (single prize)
- App must run **primarily on Arbitrum**, with chain-abstracted UX (embedded wallets, social login, gas abstraction, invisible bridging, account abstraction).
- Winner encouraged to apply to **Arbitrum Founder House, London, July 10–12** (application-based, limited capacity).

### Magic Labs — $500 (single prize)
- Integrate **Magic's embedded wallet** for the best/most creative onboarding + wallet UX.

## Stackability (the economic shape)
One well-built app can win a **main track** AND the **Arbitrum $2,000** AND the
**Magic $500**, because the bonuses are judged separately. Best-case from a single
build: e.g. UA Track 1st ($2,500) + Arbitrum ($2,000) + Magic ($500) = **$5,000**.
