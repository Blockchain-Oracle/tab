# Pitch-deck prompt — Tab (5 slides)

Paste this to your deck AI (Gamma, Beautiful.ai, Canva AI, or a slide-generating agent).
It produces a **5-slide** hackathon pitch deck for **Tab**. Keep every slide to ONE idea,
short lines, and NO jargon on-screen (the tech lives in the speaker notes).

---

## Global brief
- **Product:** Tab — a payment product. One balance, two ways to spend it: people check out
  with just an email; their AI agents pay their own way (on a budget you set and can pull).
- **Motto / closing line:** **"Invisible payments — for you, and for your AI."**
- **Audience:** hackathon judges. They score: UX excellence (40%), prominent/innovative use of
  Particle Universal Accounts + EIP-7702 (30%), adoption potential (20%), polish (10%). Lean
  the deck into UX + the agent-economy novelty.
- **Tone:** confident, plain-spoken, human. Show, don't jargon-dump.
- **Style:** clean, light, premium (Stripe × Linear). Lots of whitespace. One warm accent
  color. NO dark-purple "crypto" gradients, no glowing circuit boards, no chrome robots, no
  coin/chain logos, no wall of bullet points. Big type, one visual per slide.
- **Consistency:** the name "Tab" appears on every slide (small wordmark). Spell everything
  correctly.

---

## Slide 1 — The problem
- **Headline:** "Paying online is still broken. And now your AI can't pay at all."
- **Body (max 3 short lines):** People juggle wallets, cards, logins. AI agents hit a paywall
  and just… stop. The agent economy is here — but agents can't spend.
- **Visual:** a person tangled in wallet/card/login clutter on one side; an AI agent frozen at
  a locked gate ("Payment required") on the other.
- **Speaker note:** set up "why now" — AI agents are doing real work but can't transact.

## Slide 2 — The solution: Tab
- **Headline:** "Tab — invisible payments, for you and your AI."
- **Body:** You check out with just an email — no wallets, no cards, no chains. Your AI agents
  pay their own way — on a budget you set and can pull anytime. One balance. Anyone, or
  anything, can pay from it.
- **Visual:** the hero image — one glowing balance in the center feeding two payers (a person
  paying by email on the left; an AI agent auto-paying on a leash on the right).
- **Speaker note:** this is the "aha" — introduce the two hero moments you'll demo.

## Slide 3 — How it works (invisible machinery)
- **Headline:** "One balance. Any chain. Humans and agents."
- **Body (3 simple steps, icon each):** (1) Log in with an email → it becomes one smart wallet
  that works across every chain, no gas, no bridges. (2) You tap to pay, or your agent
  auto-pays when it hits a charge. (3) A budget + kill switch keep the agent on a leash.
- **Visual:** a simple 3-step flow. Under it, a small "powered by" line: Particle Universal
  Accounts + EIP-7702 · x402 · Arbitrum · Magic.
- **Speaker note:** THIS is the 30% judging axis — say plainly that EIP-7702 turns the email
  wallet into a chain-abstracted Universal Account, and that funding the agent's float is a
  real cross-chain move via the UA. Keep the slide itself non-technical.

## Slide 4 — Why Tab wins
- **Headline:** "The wallet the agent economy is missing."
- **Body (3 punchy points):** (1) **Nobody auto-pays x402 with real controls** — existing
  tools have no spend caps, no kill switch, no monitoring (or are locked to one cloud). Tab is
  the safety-railed, works-with-any-agent one. (2) **Invisible UX** — feels like a normal
  product, not a crypto app. (3) **It compounds** — every AI agent is a potential Tab.
- **Visual:** a simple comparison (Tab ✅ vs "SDKs you must code in / cloud-locked / no caps"),
  or a "for people AND agents" market arrow.
- **Speaker note:** adoption (20%) + novelty. Name the open niche you found.

## Slide 5 — Vision + demo + ask
- **Headline:** "Money should move as freely as everything else online."
- **Body:** Show the live demo in one line each: "Pay with just an email → done." and "Watch
  an AI agent hit a paywall, Tab pays it, and yank the leash." Then: what's next (the roadmap
  in one line) and the team.
- **Closing line, big and centered:** **"Invisible payments — for you, and for your AI."**
- **Visual:** a clean end card with the Tab wordmark + the motto.
- **Speaker note:** end on the motto; invite them to try the demo.

---

## Output format
Produce exactly 5 slides in order. For each: a short headline, ≤3 short body lines (or 3
icon-points), one visual suggestion, and a 1–2 sentence speaker note. Do not add extra slides.
Keep the whole deck skimmable in 90 seconds.
