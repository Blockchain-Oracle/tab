🏆 Universal Accounts Track
Particle Network logoSupported by Particle Network
1st Place($2,500)
2nd Place($2,000)
3rd Prize($1,500)

Build with Particle Network's Universal Accounts SDK in EIP-7702 mode. The user's EOA becomes a chain-abstracted account in place—one login, one balance, transactions on any chain with any asset. No new address, no migration, no smart-account deployment. Just upgrade the EOA and ship.

Requirements:

    Must use Universal Accounts SDK in EIP-7702 mode.
    At least one cross-chain operation moving value via UA.
    Functional demo (deployed or runnable locally).

Arbitrum “Road to Open House London” Bounty
Arbitrum logoSupported by Arbitrum
Prize pool($2,000)

Leading up to Arbitrum's flagship Founder House residency hosted by Encode House in London, Arbitrum will independently award the best dApp in this hackathon with a single $2,000 prize, as long as the application and its components are deployed on the Arbitrum network.

Build a consumer app where Arbitrum powers the experience behind the scenes, but the user never has to think about wallets, gas fees, bridges, or what chain they’re using.

Projects must run primarily on Arbitrum and should use chain-abstracted UX patterns like embedded wallets, social login, gas abstraction, invisible bridging, or account abstraction.
Example ideas:

    AI apps with invisible onchain payments
    Social or gaming apps with walletless onboarding
    Consumer payment or loyalty apps
    Cross-chain apps where Arbitrum acts as the backend settlement layer
    Mobile-first apps with seamless UX

Judges will look for:

    UX excellence (30%)
    Creativity (30%)
    Adoption potential (20%)
    Execution quality (20%).

Judges may use the overall app quality as a tie-breaker

The best projects will feel less like “crypto apps” and more like normal consumer products that just happen to run onchain.

Track Selection, Rules & Judging
Particle Network logo
Supported by Particle Network
Participants must submit their project to one main track:

Universal Accounts Track: Build a dApp that prominently uses Particle Network’s Universal Accounts in EIP-7702 mode, in combination with a supported wallet provider, for a chain-agnostic UX. 

Or General Track: Build a Web3 application with exceptional user experience in any domain.

Projects on this track, regardless of their track-winning status, can also earn additional bonus prizes through the Arbitrum ($2000) and Magic Labs ($500) bounties. These bounties will be independently judged and awarded and, as such, will follow their own criteria and standards.

Besides the two main tracks and the General track’s subtracks, there are two additional bounties that participants can choose to pursue, regardless of their track choice.

All winning projects in this track may, at Particle Network's discretion, be selected for incubation. 
Criteria 

Judges will seek the most seamless user experience, with particular emphasis on chain-agnostic features powered by Universal Accounts + EIP-7702. They will focus on UX excellence (40%), Prominent/innovative use of Universal Accounts + EIP-7702 (30%), adoption potential (20%), and technical quality/polish (10%).
Judging note:

Main track winners will be selected by the hackathon jury, while subtracks and bonus challenges will be judged independently by the relevant partner teams. Teams may therefore be considered for multiple prizes depending
Rules:

    All work original and created during the hackathon. Plagiarism = disqualification.
